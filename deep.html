About Us

Welcome to EduTech Innovations, a leading company in educational technology. We specialize in creating cutting-edge products designed to inspire and educate learners of all ages, from 3 to 18 years old. Our diverse range of products includes:

  • VinciBot: A programmable robot that introduces kids to coding and robotics.
  • ClassVR: Immersive virtual reality experiences for interactive learning.
  • TaleBot: A storytelling robot that encourages creativity and language skills.
  • ProSet: Advanced STEM kits for hands-on learning and experimentation.
  • Wizefloor: An interactive floor projection system for collaborative learning.

Our mission is to transform education by integrating technology into the learning process, making it more engaging, interactive, and effective. Join us in shaping the future of education!
